Superman

The Man of Steel

Superman is one of the world's greatest superheroes, famous for his courage, kindness, and incredible powers.

Origin

Born as Kal-El on the planet Krypton, Superman was sent to Earth as a baby before his home planet was destroyed.

Superpowers

He possesses super strength, flight, heat vision, x-ray vision, super speed, and near invulnerability.

Mission

Superman uses his extraordinary abilities to protect innocent people and fight for truth, justice, and hope.

Weakness

His greatest weakness is Kryptonite, a radioactive material from his home planet that weakens him.
